Josh Aarons

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Economics at University of California San Diego. My research applies applied microeconomics tools to environmental questions. Currently I am studying competition and consumer behavior in the EV charging market and the impacts of noise pollution on student performance. I earned a BA in Economics from Carleton College.

I expect to graduate in June 2027 and will be on the academic job market in the fall of 2026.
